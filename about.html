About Mistry Shop

Mistry Shop is a neighbourhood marketplace dedicated to delivering high-quality natural and organic products at fair prices. We combine traditional knowledge with modern sourcing and quality checks to bring you trusted groceries, wellness products, and everyday essentials.

Founded with the aim to support local producers and make healthy choices accessible, our focus is on freshness, transparency, and excellent customer service.

Our Mission

To source and deliver wholesome, responsibly produced products that support healthier lives and stronger local communities.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted local marketplace where customers choose quality, ethics, and sustainability — one purchase at a time.
